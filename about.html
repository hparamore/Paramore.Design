About

Hunter Paramore

I'm a product designer with 12 years of experience building digital products — from early-stage startups to consumer apps serving hundreds of thousands of users. I studied art with an emphasis on design, and I've spent my career in the space where design meets product meets engineering.

I've worked at UX design studios shipping projects for dozens of clients, led design for large-scale consumer products, and built my own apps from scratch. That range means I can see the full picture — user needs, technical constraints, business goals — and move between them fluently.

I think in systems. I obsess over the details that make a product feel right. I dig into user feedback to find root causes, not just symptoms. And I believe the best products come from people who care more about the experience than the metrics — because good UX and good business aren't at odds when you're building something people genuinely want to use.

Whether I'm embedded on a fast-moving team, working directly with a founder on their vision, or building something of my own — I bring the same thing: a relentless drive to make the product better, and the ability to do whatever it takes to get there.
